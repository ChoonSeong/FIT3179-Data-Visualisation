2018 Criminal Cases in India

Visualisation by: Choon Seong

India is known as one of the world's most populous countries. Many of the people in India face tough times because of poverty and some rules that can be unfair. Not everyone gets the right kind of education, and that causes increment of criminal cases in India. Therefore it is a valuable exercise to examine these issues in an Indian context to gain insights into regional variations and challenges faced by law enforcement.
